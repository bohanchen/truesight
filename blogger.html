A GIRL JUST LOVES BEAUTY MAKE UP

Q: How long have you been in America so far?

It has been five and half years


Q: How old were you when you start using cosmetics?

It began when I was a sophomore in high school.


Q: What is the difference between American and Chinese make up?

I think Chinese girl’s make up is less obvious than American girls’ make up. But it really a personal preference thing.

Q: How much money do you spend on your cosmetics?

I never actually count the number. I don’t think there is a fixed number every month. I will buy new cosmetics when there is a big sale or I need something specific.


Q: What do you miss the most about your hometown?

The food and my family.


Q: How do you think the negative news of Chinese students?

I think these negative articles are just special cases. Yet it becomes really influential when it is all over the news.

Q: Do you think the general public have any misunderstanding of Chinese students who study aboard?

They think we are just partying and playing every day, but they don’t know how much pressure we are facing at school. In fact, we are more struggle than students in China.


Q: What is the most important value you believe that Chinese students should have?

Students should not regret whatever the path they choose.


Q: Do you want to go back to China or stay in America?

Currently, I want to stay in America.
